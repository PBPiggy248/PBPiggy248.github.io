Risky Peanut Butter

  • Time: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Peanuts 10 Cups
Butter 6 Sticks

Step 1: Place peanuts and butter in mixing bowl and squeeze inbetween fingers for 10 minutes.
Step 2: Lay mixture on aluminum foil and microwave for 5 minutes.

Mac and cheesecake

  • Time: 30 Minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Cheesecake 1 Cake
Mac and cheese 6 Cups
Flour 1 Cup
Water 2 Cups

Step 1: Place flour, water, and cheesecake in blender and blend on high for five minutes.
Step 2: Hand mix blended ingredients with mac and cheese.
Step 3: PLace mixture in deep oven pan and spread evenly.
Step 4: Cook in oven for 10-20 minutes.

Warm Water

  • Time: 5 Minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Water 1 Cup
Lighter Fluid 5 Table spoons

Step 1: Place water in a glass.
Step 2: Pour lighter fluid along the surface of the water.
Step 3: Light one match and place against the surface of the water until lighter fluid ignites.
